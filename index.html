You Pick Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2, 2019

We passionately believe that each person’s data is theirs to be controlled and protected, not something to be mined and packaged to Big Data in order to sell more designer underwear.

That said, You Pick never collects or stores information about you. Searching for restaurants occurs locally on your own device and while You Pick may use—at your discretion—your location information to provide you with delicious dining options, we never see that information. If you want us to join you for dinner, please contact us at the email address below.

PERSONALLY-IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

You Pick does not gather or transmit any Personally-Identifiable Information outside of your personal device. Any user interface preferences that you set are stored in your device’s local database.
